First-use guide

1

Open the extension

Look at the top-right of Chrome, just to the right of the URL bar. Click the extensions icon, then pin or open Stack Highlighter.

www.linkedin.com/jobs
2

Control highlights from the side panel

Use ON/OFF to control keyword highlights on the current page. Try it on LinkedIn.

3

Manage your tech stack

Add: select a word on the page and add it from the side panel.

Delete: click x to remove a stack.

Highlight: click a stack chip to jump to its match.

Open the Advanced options guide for JSON edits.

ON OFF
Enable or disable anytime.

The ON/OFF switch in the side panel controls highlighting across pages. Closing the side panel does not turn highlights off; opening it again turns Stack Highlighter ON.
